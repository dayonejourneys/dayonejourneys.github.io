What a Prompt Looks Like

Every prompt includes the task, the time saved, copy-paste text with brackets, an example output, and a pro tip.

Prompt #2 — HR Manager's AI Playbook
Write a performance review in under 10 minutes
⏱ Saves 45-60 min per review · Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini
You are an HR professional helping a manager write a formal performance review. Here are the manager's raw notes: [PASTE RAW NOTES HERE] Employee: [NAME] | Role: [JOB TITLE] Rating: [EXCEEDS / MEETS / BELOW EXPECTATIONS] Period: [REVIEW PERIOD] Write a 300-400 word professional narrative using concrete examples from the notes. Use observable behavior only — no character judgments. Flag any language that references protected characteristics or isn't tied to a specific example.
A polished 300-word performance narrative with specific behavioral examples, a legal language audit identifying any risky phrases, and suggested replacements — ready to review and send in 10 minutes.
